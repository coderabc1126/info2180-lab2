Story of How I became a Lifeguard

October 1, 2022 by Antonia Curtis

Growing up, I always loved the water and swimming. I did swimming at both my prep and high school and I was even on my high school's swim team. I was apart of the swim club and did swimming every friday. I also attended swim meets to represent my school.

Although I was in water and swimming. I didn't like competing. I realize that I didn't want to racing aganist others and would rather persons just haave fun and play in the water. This is why I became a lifeguard. I ensure that persons are safe while they have fun.
